# Indian Cuisine

![Indian food spread](/images/indian-hero.jpg)

## Flavor Profile

Indian cuisine is built around **complex spice blends** (masalas) and varies dramatically by region. Northern cuisine features creamy curries and tandoor-cooked breads, while Southern cuisine emphasizes rice, coconut, and seafood.

Key flavors include **cumin, coriander, turmeric, ginger, and garam masala**. Many dishes use **ghee** (clarified butter) and **yogurt** for richness.
