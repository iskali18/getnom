# Mexican Cuisine

![Mexican food spread](/images/mexican-hero.jpg)

## Flavor Profile

Mexican cuisine is built on **corn, beans, and chilies** with regional variations throughout the country. It balances **bright acidity** (lime, tomatoes), **earthy depth** (beans, chilies), and **fresh elements** (cilantro, onions).

True Mexican food is more complex than Tex-Mex, with diverse regional specialties and ancient cooking techniques.

## ðŸŸ¢ Beginner Dishes

### Tacos al Pastor
Marinated pork cooked on a vertical spit, served on soft corn tortillas with pineapple.

**What to expect:** Sweet-savory pork, simple and delicious

---

### Quesadilla
Grilled flour tortilla with melted cheese (often with chicken or beef added).

**What to expect:** Familiar, comforting, like a Mexican grilled cheese

---

### Guacamole & Chips
Fresh avocado dip with lime, cilantro, onions, and tomatoes.

**What to expect:** Creamy, bright, perfect introduction to fresh Mexican flavors

---

## ðŸŸ¡ Intermediate Dishes

### Carnitas
Slow-braised pork that's tender inside and crispy outside. Often served in tacos.

**What to expect:** Rich, flavorful pork with textural contrast

---

### Enchiladas
Rolled tortillas filled with meat or cheese, covered in chili sauce and cheese.

**What to expect:** Saucy, comforting, moderately spicy

---

### Mole Poblano
Dark, complex sauce with chilies, chocolate, spices. Served over chicken.

**What to expect:** Deep, slightly sweet, not chocolate-forward despite cocoa
