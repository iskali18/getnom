# Thai Cuisine

![Thai food spread](/images/thai-hero.jpg)

## Flavor Profile

Thai cuisine is built around the balance of **four core flavors**: sweet, sour, salty, and spicy. Almost every dish plays with this harmony using ingredients like fish sauce, lime, palm sugar, and chilies.

The cuisine emphasizes **fresh herbs** (basil, cilantro, mint), **aromatics** (lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime), and **coconut milk** in many dishes.

Start here if you're new to Thai food. These dishes have familiar textures and balanced flavors.

### Pad Thai
Stir-fried rice noodles with egg, tofu or shrimp, peanuts, and bean sprouts. Sweet-savory with a hint of lime.

**What to expect:** Soft noodles, crunchy peanuts, mild spice (can request none)

![Pad Thai](/images/pad-thai.jpg)

---

### Chicken Satay
Grilled chicken skewers marinated in coconut and spices, served with peanut sauce.

**What to expect:** Tender, slightly sweet, with creamy peanut dipping sauce

![Chicken Satay](/images/chicken-satay.jpg)

### Massaman Curry

## ðŸŸ¡ Intermediate Dishes

Ready to explore more traditional Thai flavors? These introduce stronger herbs and spice.

### Green Curry
Coconut curry with Thai basil, bamboo shoots, and green chilies. Aromatic and moderately spicy.

**What to expect:** Creamy with bright herbal notes, medium heat

---

### Drunken Noodles (Pad Kee Mao)
Wide rice noodles stir-fried with Thai basil, chilies, and vegetables. Bold and savory.

**What to expect:** Slightly charred flavor, chewy noodles, noticeable spice

---

### Tom Yum Soup
Hot and sour soup with lemongrass, galangal, lime, and chilies. Can be ordered with shrimp or chicken.

**What to expect:** Bright, tangy, spicyâ€”very aromatic

## ðŸ”´ Adventurous Dishes

For those who want the full Thai experience. These showcase regional specialties and bold flavors.

### Larb (Laap)
Minced meat salad from Northern Thailand with lime, fish sauce, toasted rice powder, and fresh herbs.

**What to expect:** Intense lime flavor, textural contrast, very herbal

---

### Som Tam (Green Papaya Salad)
Shredded unripe papaya pounded with chilies, lime, fish sauce, and peanuts. Can be very spicy.

**What to expect:** Crunchy, sour, salty, and potentially very hot

---

### Khao Soi
Northern Thai curry noodle soup with crispy fried noodles on top. Rich coconut broth with curry paste.

**What to expect:** Complex curry flavor, mix of soft and crispy textures
