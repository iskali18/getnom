# Vietnamese Cuisine

![Vietnamese food spread](/images/vietnamese-hero.jpg)

## Flavor Profile

Vietnamese cuisine is known for being **light, fresh, and balanced**. It uses abundant fresh herbs, fish sauce, and lime to create bright, clean flavors.

Unlike Thai cuisine, Vietnamese food tends to be **less spicy** and more subtle, with dishes often served with herbs and vegetables on the side for customization.

## ðŸŸ¢ Beginner Dishes

### Pho (Beef Noodle Soup)
Aromatic broth with rice noodles, beef, and fresh herbs. Vietnam's national dish.

**What to expect:** Light, savory broth with tender meat and soft noodles

---

### Spring Rolls (Goi Cuon)
Fresh rice paper rolls with shrimp, pork, herbs, and vermicelli. Served with peanut sauce.

**What to expect:** Cool, refreshing, crunchy vegetables

---

### Banh Mi
Vietnamese sandwich with pate, cold cuts, pickled vegetables, and cilantro on crispy baguette.

**What to expect:** Crunchy bread, savory-tangy filling, fresh herbs

---

## ðŸŸ¡ Intermediate Dishes

### Bun Cha
Grilled pork with rice noodles, herbs, and sweet-sour dipping sauce.

**What to expect:** Charred meat, fresh greens, interactive eating experience

---

### Com Tam (Broken Rice)
Rice dish with grilled pork chop, egg, pickled vegetables, and fish sauce dressing.

**What to expect:** Textured rice, savory-sweet flavors

---

### Cao Lau
Hoi An specialty noodles with pork, greens, and crispy wonton.

**What to expect:** Chewy noodles, smoky flavor, regional authenticity

### Bun Bo Hue
Spicy beef noodle soup from Central Vietnam. More assertive than pho.

**What to expect:** Rich, spicy broth with lemongrass, thicker noodles

---

### Banh Xeo
Crispy turmeric crepe filled with pork, shrimp, and bean sprouts.

**What to expect:** Crunchy exterior, wrap in lettuce with herbs

---

### Ca Kho To
Caramelized fish in clay pot with fish sauce and sugar.

**What to expect:** Sweet-savory, strong fish flavor, very authentic
